Mosasaurus

The Mosasaur was a oceanic predator, approximately over 40 feet long and having a fossil record over the span of 27 million years.
Its was discovered in 1764, by lieutenant Jean Baptiste Drouin.

Fun facts

  • The name Mosasaurus means: "Lizard of the Meuse River".
  • The largergest discovered Mosasaurus had a length of up to 56 feet long!
  • The Mosasaurus spent most of its time near the ocean surface, much like modern whales do, since they breathed air in a similar fashion.
