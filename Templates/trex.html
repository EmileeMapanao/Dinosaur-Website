Tyrannosaurus Rex

The Tyrannosaurus Rex was a large carnivoruos dinosaur that lived during the Cretaceous period, 68 million years ago.
They're famously aggreasive hunting was aided by their keen sense of smell.
Its diet consisted of primarily herbivorous dinosuars including the Edmontosarus and Triceratops.

Fun facts

  • Tyrannosaurus Rex could deliver a bite force up to 6 tons
  • Tyrannosaurus Rex literally means "king of the tyrant lizards"
  • Tyrannosaurus Rex grew up to the size of a school bus
